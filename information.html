Fastlane Tabs

Press the default shortcut (Ctrl+Alt+S) to open the tab switch dialog and search for the tab you need—much like Chrome or Edge’s quick search. You can also close tabs directly from this dialog. If you prefer a different shortcut, you can change it in Firefox’s add-on settings.

Bindings in tab dialog

Tab or down arrow: Move selection down

Shift+tab or arrow up: Move selection up

Enter: Switch to selected tab

Ctrl+delete: Close selected tab

You can also select a tab by clicking it with your mouse.
